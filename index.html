Services

Consultations

Half and full hour consultations are available to assess your case and discuss what potential resolutions are available. You will be provided with a thorough overview during the consultation, so you leave feeling confident, and more knowledgeable about resolving your legal matter.

Opinion Letters

I provide clients with opinion letters for criminal proceedings that involve immigration consequences. A legal opinion on your immigration consequences can assist in changing the outcome of your criminal proceedings, and help you attain a better result.

Full Case

I will assess your case to provide a quote for legal services you require. I will handle the case from start to end and help you achieve a desirable outcome.
